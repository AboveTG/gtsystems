# GT Systems

## Ground Truth Analysis Platform

GT Systems is an independent analysis platform designed to help users examine language framing, persuasion techniques, emotional triggers, and narrative construction in news articles, social media posts, transcripts, and other forms of media.

The objective is not to determine factual truth, but to identify how information is presented and whether persuasive language, framing techniques, or emotional appeals are being used.

---

## Current Features

### Article & Text Analysis

Users can paste:

* News articles
* Social media posts
* Political statements
* Press releases
* Speeches
* Opinion pieces
* AI-generated content

The system analyzes:

* Emotional language
* Persuasive framing
* Narrative construction
* Authority appeals
* Fear-based language
* Urgency cues
* Linguistic influence techniques

Results include:

* Persuasion Score
* Detected Techniques
* Summary
* Key Observations
* Framing Notes

## Design Philosophy

GT Systems is built around a simple principle:

Information should be examined, not merely consumed.

The platform focuses on language patterns, framing structures, and persuasion techniques while encouraging independent evaluation of information.

---

## Disclaimer

GT Systems does not determine objective truth and should not be interpreted as a fact-checking system.

Analysis results represent linguistic and rhetorical observations generated by AI models and should be used as one input among many when evaluating information.
